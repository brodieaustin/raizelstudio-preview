Raizel Studio is a shared space in southeast Evanston for creative individuals. Raizel Designs offers people individualized attention to haircuts, styles, and up-dos in an inspired environment. Anna Palumbo is a licensed barber with a passion for short cuts for men and women. Visual artist Ellie Hazlett uses the space for her multimedia work.

  • RAIZEL DESIGN / Risa Posner

    In 2013, Risa Posner started Raizel Designs after working 11 years in the hair industry and 5 years in visual display art. In 2018, Risa opened Raizel Studios where she continues to provide these services in a creative and collaborative environment.

  • Anna Palumbo

    Originally trained as a hairstylist in 2003, Anna went on to get licensed in the art of barbering and moved into Raizel Studio in 2018. She offers everything from straight razor shaves to long layer haircuts, with a special passion for short hairstyles for both men and women.
